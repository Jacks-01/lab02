About Me!

My name is Jack Stubblefield, I am 21 years old and have just started my journey in software development

I graduated from Linn-Mar High School in 2019, then proceeded to Kirkwood Community College, where I majored in social work with the goal of getting my masters marriage and family therapy. I decided it wasn't what I wanted to do long-term so I enrolled at DeltaV to learn software development.

Before I changed career fields I worked at Tanager Place as a youth service worker, then I took another job working as a housing case manager at Willis Dady Homeless shelter. Once I realized that social work wasn't for me, I accepted an internal position doing data analytics.

I don't have any specific career goals yet, but I am exploring options in software development. I want more experience and new languages under my toolbelt before I make and long-term goals.
